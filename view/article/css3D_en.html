I present you David DeSandro's work on 3D volume with CSS.

3D exists since a long time in the web with canvas, Three.js (WebGL) or Flash. Since 2009, developers have 3D CSS transformation module. This is a revival for front-ends who until then were learning a third language to give some volume to their site.

This tutorial, proposes you the construction of a 3D volume, step by step. The beginning is a simple perspective to an interactive 3D carousel.

Here is the link of the tutorial. Once mastered, it will give you a new perspective for your site.

https://3dtransforms.desandro.com/
